How it works

One thread between services. Not another service.

OTOS Continuity does not replace clinical or partner systems. It holds a light continuity thread around agreed touchpoints so the person does not disappear between them.

View planPartner routes
02

Pre-pilot version

Limited functionality for proof.

The demo build needs enough capability to handle consent, tokens, partner touchpoints, receipts, drift signals and a learning report.

Full pilot version

A wider continuity layer.

The future pilot can become more sophisticated, but only after the pre-pilot proves the pattern and partners agree what is safe and useful.

03

The loop

Spot, log, receipt, review.

The core action is simple: a partner sees an agreed signal, logs it, receives a receipt, and the right people can review the thread.

  • No hidden clinical decision.
  • No record merger.
  • No vague handoff.

04

Roles stay clear

Continuity does not mean ownership transfer.

Partner

logs or confirms agreed touchpoint

OTOS layer

holds the signal and receipt

Human reviewer

checks drift or next-step issue

Clinical team

keeps clinical decisions where they belong
