Ripple effect

One small signal can change the next week.

The ripple argument is not that OTOS magically solves everything. It is that one missed connection can create repeated cost and crisis — and one timely signal may stop the thread snapping.

View planView numbers
02

The human ripple

A missed ADHD review does not stay clinical.

It can become alcohol crisis, family strain, work instability, A&E, detox, shame, relapse and repeat service use.

The system ripple

Costs move between budgets.

When continuity fails, costs do not disappear. They move into emergency, addiction, justice, housing, family and public-health pressure.

03

The demonstrator question

Can one signal alter the chain?

The pre-pilot tests whether an agreed touchpoint — bedside, keyworker, ADHD route, community support — changes what partners can see and do next.

  • A signal lands.
  • The loop is aware.
  • Silence becomes reviewable.
