A course attended is not a small thing. It is proof of contact.

02

Why RCE matters

Attendance is a continuity signal.

A course, session or wellbeing interaction can become more than an isolated event. It can show the person is still in the thread.

The immediate ask

Help define useful non-clinical signals.

For the demonstrator, RCE-type contact could help partners understand what counts as positive engagement, not just crisis or non-attendance.

03

The useful signal

Contact is data when handled carefully.

A simple attendance or engagement note can show that someone remains connected without turning community support into surveillance.

  • Positive contact.
  • Human engagement.
  • A safe signal for review.
