RCE Wellbeing Hub

Post-medication, I could finally use the support.

RCE matters because it sits in the real waiting-list pathway. Once ADHD medication was working, courses and support became usable — and attendance itself became a sign that I was still connected, still trying and still reachable.

Why RCE matters

This is what holding looks like after treatment starts working.

RCE is not a clinical ADHD service and should not be asked to become one. Its value is different: it gives people structure, learning, contact and a place to keep engaging while the formal system is still moving.

The immediate ask

Help define useful non-clinical signals.

For the demonstrator, RCE-type contact could help partners understand what counts as positive engagement, not just crisis or non-attendance.

03

The useful signal

Attendance is a positive continuity signal.

For the demonstrator, RCE does not need access to clinical records. The question is simpler: can agreed course attendance, missed sessions or micro-check-ins help show whether someone is still connected, drifting or ready for the next support step?

  • Positive contact.
  • Course engagement.
  • A safe signal for human review.
