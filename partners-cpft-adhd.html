The clinical route

The ADHD Team owns the ADHD decisions. Full stop.

OTOS is not proposing to assess ADHD, prescribe medication, prioritise the queue or decide who gets seen first. That belongs with clinicians.

The demonstrator asks a narrower question: can addiction and ADHD partners safely identify a small group where unresolved ADHD may be driving repeated addiction crisis, and define what the right clinical route should be?

OTOS keeps the route visible while that question is being worked out: referral logged, wait visible, drift detected, human review triggered.

Blueprint view: pathway visibility only. Clinical decisions stay with partners.

The thing I need you to hear
