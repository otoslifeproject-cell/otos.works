01 · Not a theory

A lived reality.

For thirty-five years, drinking worked before it nearly killed me. That is the point. The question nobody asked early enough was why it worked so well.

02 · The collapse

The system saw what was on fire. It did not ask what was burning.

Addiction, hospital admissions, crisis contact, rehab, community support and family pressure all became visible. The underlying ADHD was documented but not connected quickly enough to change the trajectory.

03

03 · The loop

The recovery work could not hold until the brain underneath could use it.

Therapy, rehab, CGL, family, effort and structure all mattered. But once the ADHD was treated, the same recovery work finally started to land. That is why the demonstrator is built around sequence and continuity.

Boundaries

What OTOS is - and what it is not.

This remains a private partner briefing and pre-pilot demonstrator frame, not a live clinical service. The purpose is to make the route visible enough for partners to correct, test and safely scope.

OTOS does not

  • diagnose, prescribe or triage clinically
  • replace NHS, local authority, CGL, CRS, GP, charity or partner records
  • write into NHS systems or create hidden clinical responsibility
  • change how any partner service runs
  • turn modelling into guaranteed outcomes

OTOS does

  • log agreed non-clinical touchpoints
  • show whether handoffs landed, stalled or disappeared
  • surface drift signals for human review
  • hold a tokenised continuity thread
  • help partners test whether the ADHD-and-addiction overlap is visible locally
