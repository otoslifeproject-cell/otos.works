Founder story

The lived experience is the design specification.

This page carries the personal truth. The homepage and partner pages should stay concise, but the deeper story needs to remain available for anyone who needs to understand why this exists.

02

The pattern

The system treated what was on fire.

Alcohol was the visible emergency. ADHD was the underlying condition that kept shaping the fire. I did not understand that until treatment changed and the whole pattern became visible.

The turning point

The letter is the receipt.

The clinical correspondence does not endorse OTOS. It confirms the case-specific link that makes the demonstrator question impossible for me to ignore.

03

The brutal truth

Without private continuity, I do not know where I would be.

My parents paid privately at the point where the public route was not keeping me safe. I believe I could have ended up dead, in prison, or back inside repeated crisis care without that intervention.

  • This is not blame.
  • It is not melodrama.
  • It is the risk pattern my life was showing.
