Pre-pilot demonstrator

Small. Surgical. Enough to prove or stop.

The pre-pilot is a funded, bounded Continuity build for a small cohort. It is not the full pilot. It exists to gather proof with minimum disruption before anyone is asked for a bigger commitment.

02

Immediate phase

The pre-pilot demonstrator.

A small cohort, likely around 50 agreed participants, is identified from existing partner visibility. The build only needs to support consent, touchpoints, receipts, drift signals and reporting for the proof exercise.

Future phase

The full Continuity pilot.

If the evidence is strong and partners are open, a larger pilot can be scoped with fuller onboarding, workflows, dashboards, governance and investment.

03

The practical demo

Minimum viable Continuity, not a full platform.

The demo build needs to be safe and useful, but deliberately limited. It proves whether the thread matters before asking anyone to fund the larger future system.

  • Consent and participant token.
  • Partner touchpoint logging.
  • Simple receipt and drift reporting.
  • Human review and learning report.
