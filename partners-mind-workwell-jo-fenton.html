The role

Not clinical ownership. Human continuity.

Mind could help shape the non-clinical support language around waiting, shame, overwhelm and disengagement. The value is not another referral machine. The value is knowing whether someone is still connected to a real human support route while the clinical pathway catches up.

Support

Boundary
