GP · RTC · QbTech route

Route options. Not OTOS decisions.

The GP and assessment route is one of the places where people can become confused, blocked or financially forced into private continuity. OTOS does not decide the route; it makes the route visible.

Discuss the demonstratorView evidence
02

The issue

People can fall between route rules.

In my case, Shared Care uncertainty, private diagnosis/treatment history and NHS medication-review status created confusion at the exact point continuity mattered.

The demonstrator ask

Clarify the route, do not own it.

The pre-pilot can help identify where route confusion is creating risk, while clinical and prescribing responsibility stays with the appropriate services.

03

What can be tested

Does the route break continuity?

The demonstrator should record whether people understand their next ADHD step, whether a review is pending, and whether addiction risk escalates while the route remains unclear.

  • Route clarity.
  • Review status.
  • Risk escalation while waiting.
