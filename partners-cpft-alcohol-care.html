CPFT Alcohol Care Team

The visible crisis is often where the missing thread can start.

Alcohol Care sees the point where a person is medically present, honest enough to be seen, and still at risk of disappearing again after discharge.

Discuss the demonstratorView Addenbrooke’s route
02

The immediate ask

A small bedside signal.

If one of the agreed cohort presents, the demonstrator asks whether one confirmation can alert the continuity thread without adding meaningful workload.

Clinical boundary

No ADHD diagnosis at the bedside.

The alcohol-care role is not to assess ADHD. It is to make the touchpoint visible to the agreed continuity loop.

03

The signal

Receipt beats memory.

A receipted touchpoint means partners do not have to rely on chance, memory or someone manually chasing the pathway after discharge.

  • Logged touchpoint.
  • Known cohort.
  • Agreed partners notified.
