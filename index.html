The point of this site

One small yes: a conversation.

The aim of this portal is not to win a public argument, launch procurement, or ask anyone to take blame. It is to secure light partner openness to discuss a small, funded pre-pilot demonstrator so I can take real partner interest into Local Authority and financing conversations.

The proof spine

The personal and clinical case now meet.

My case is no longer only a lived-experience story. A consultant psychiatrist has now put in writing that my excess alcohol use was linked to inadequately managed ADHD, and that effective ADHD treatment matters to reduce the risk of returning to excess alcohol use. That is the reason for the demonstrator.

The letter block

The missing link is now written down.

The letter should be used carefully. It is not a weapon against partners. It is a clinical proof-point showing why the ADHD-and-addiction overlap needs testing locally, in a bounded and partner-safe way.

  • Show the redacted letter, not a tabloid document.
  • Make the gap visible without alleging blame.
  • Use it to justify the question: how many others are caught in the same pattern?
