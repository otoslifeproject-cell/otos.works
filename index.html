01 · In one paragraph

A non-clinical continuity layer between services.

OTOS Continuity™ sits between the services this cohort already touches - addiction, ADHD, GP, community recovery, hospital and wellbeing support - and makes the journey between them visible, receipted and held by a named human, so people stop disappearing in the gaps.

The system already had the pieces. Nobody connected them while it mattered.

02 · The demonstrator

12 weeks. Around 50 people. Small enough to stop cleanly.

This is not a new clinical service. It is a tightly bounded demonstrator to find the cohort, hold the thread and test whether earlier ADHD recognition changes the addiction and recovery pathway.

Find the cohort

CGL, CRS, CPFT and relevant partners help define a small overlap group already visible to services.

Hold the thread

Agreed touchpoints, handoff receipts, attendance signals and drift are logged as continuity signals.

Let clinicians decide

Clinical ownership, diagnosis, prescribing and risk decisions stay exactly where they belong.

03 · What it does

Make the missing middle visible.

The purpose is not to add noise. It is to show whether the next step happened, whether the person stayed connected, and whether silence has become a risk signal.

  • Logs handoffs between services so the next step can be seen.
  • Surfaces drift signals so silent disengagement is visible before crisis.
  • Holds a tokenised thread so continuity is possible without merging records.

04

Boundaries

What OTOS is - and what it is not.

This remains a private partner briefing and pre-pilot demonstrator frame, not a live clinical service. The purpose is to make the route visible enough for partners to correct, test and safely scope.

OTOS does not

  • diagnose, prescribe or triage clinically
  • replace NHS, local authority, CGL, CRS, GP, charity or partner records
  • write into NHS systems or create hidden clinical responsibility
  • change how any partner service runs
  • turn modelling into guaranteed outcomes

OTOS does

  • log agreed non-clinical touchpoints
  • show whether handoffs landed, stalled or disappeared
  • surface drift signals for human review
  • hold a tokenised continuity thread
  • help partners test whether the ADHD-and-addiction overlap is visible locally
