The system already had the pieces. Nobody connected them while it mattered.

OTOS Continuity™ exists to test whether the same missed pattern is sitting inside the current addiction and recovery caseload: people already visible to services, where unresolved ADHD may be driving repeated crisis, relapse, disappearance and return.

This is not a new clinical service. It is a tightly bounded 12-week demonstrator to find the cohort, hold the thread and see whether earlier ADHD recognition changes the recovery outcome.
01 · Find the cohort

CGL / CRS and ADHD partners help identify the overlap: addiction/recovery clients where ADHD may be the hidden driver.

02 · Hold the thread

OTOS logs agreed touchpoints, handoff receipts, attendance signals and drift. Not clinical notes. Not records. Continuity signals.

03 · Let clinicians decide

The ADHD route, risk and next action stay with the clinical partners. OTOS shows where the person is in the journey.

What this is really about

Medicate the ADHD. Watch addiction support finally land.

That is the lived truth behind the demonstrator. Not magic. Not a claim of cure. The addiction work had been there for years. Therapy, rehab, CGL, support, family, effort. It started to hold properly only when the ADHD underneath was treated.

Illustrative dashboard view. Not live operational data.

Boundaries

Small enough to start. Serious enough to matter.

OTOS will not

  • diagnose ADHD
  • prescribe medication
  • prioritise or fast-track anyone independently
  • replace NHS, CGL, CRS or partner records
  • make automated clinical decisions

OTOS will

  • make agreed touchpoints visible
  • show whether handoffs landed
  • capture non-clinical engagement signals
  • surface drift before total disappearance
  • help partners test whether the pattern exists in 50 people
