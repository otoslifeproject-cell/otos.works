01 · Core idea

Treat the addiction alone, and some of us keep coming back.

Treat the ADHD underneath it, and the recovery work can finally hold. That is the lived mechanism behind this demonstrator, and CGL / CRS are central because the pattern is already visible in addiction and recovery work.

02 · Cohort question

Are there 50 people already visible where ADHD is the missed driver?

That is the first test. Not a platform test. Not a dashboard test. A real cohort question. Help define a jointly agreed group already in or around addiction and recovery services where ADHD may be unresolved, untreated or waiting invisibly in the background.

03

03 · Messaging and drift

The language matters. The nudge matters. The silence matters.

Generic reminders do not understand shame, avoidance, relapse, ADHD paralysis or the moment someone is close to disappearing. Small, human, low-pressure messages should be shaped with the people who understand the cohort.

  • Message delivered, phone active, link opened, reply sent, course attended.
  • Tiny signals that someone has not vanished.
  • If drift appears, the agreed human reviewer sees it. OTOS does not decide. People decide.

Boundaries

What OTOS is - and what it is not.

This remains a private partner briefing and pre-pilot demonstrator frame, not a live clinical service. The purpose is to make the route visible enough for partners to correct, test and safely scope.

OTOS does not

  • diagnose, prescribe or triage clinically
  • replace NHS, local authority, CGL, CRS, GP, charity or partner records
  • write into NHS systems or create hidden clinical responsibility
  • change how any partner service runs
  • turn modelling into guaranteed outcomes

OTOS does

  • log agreed non-clinical touchpoints
  • show whether handoffs landed, stalled or disappeared
  • surface drift signals for human review
  • hold a tokenised continuity thread
  • help partners test whether the ADHD-and-addiction overlap is visible locally

06 · What stays with you

The keyworker stays in control of every decision.

No ADHD diagnosis by CGL or CRS. No clinical record replacement. No hidden risk transfer. The demonstrator asks for a small agreed continuity role: help identify the cohort, shape the language, and test whether the thread can hold long enough to change the outcome.
