Local authority / public health

A cost-redirection opportunity hiding in current spend.

This is a discovery briefing - not a funding ask. OTOS Continuity™ is a bounded pre-pilot targeting adults cycling through addiction and crisis, where unresolved ADHD may be the underlying driver of repeat demand.

01 · Public health frame

This is not a new cost. It is smarter use of what is already spent.

The question is whether existing demand across addiction, crisis, emergency and community services contains an overlap cohort that could be held earlier and more coherently.

02 · Reactive vs connected

The cost does not disappear. It moves to a different budget line.

If the underlying driver is not connected, the system pays for the downstream events: relapse, emergency attendance, safeguarding pressure, justice contact, housing pressure and family breakdown.

Boundaries

What OTOS is - and what it is not.

This remains a private partner briefing and pre-pilot demonstrator frame, not a live clinical service. The purpose is to make the route visible enough for partners to correct, test and safely scope.

OTOS does not

  • diagnose, prescribe or triage clinically
  • replace NHS, local authority, CGL, CRS, GP, charity or partner records
  • write into NHS systems or create hidden clinical responsibility
  • change how any partner service runs
  • turn modelling into guaranteed outcomes

OTOS does

  • log agreed non-clinical touchpoints
  • show whether handoffs landed, stalled or disappeared
  • surface drift signals for human review
  • hold a tokenised continuity thread
  • help partners test whether the ADHD-and-addiction overlap is visible locally
