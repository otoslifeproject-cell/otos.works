02

The demo funding reality

A smaller build still costs money.

The pre-pilot requires a working Continuity layer for the agreed demo. Current expectation: roughly £90k minimum, probably closer to £150k depending on the final safe scope.

Why this matters

The pre-pilot prevents a bigger blind ask.

Without the demonstrator, the full pilot would be too large and speculative. The small proof move is the safer financial route.
